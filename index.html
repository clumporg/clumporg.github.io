What is Clump?

It is a way to store data as carbon, forever.

Think of it as cloud storage - except your data is recorded on carbon rather than on HDDs, SSDs or tapes.

Why use Clump?

It solves your long-term carbon storage and long-term data storage problems together as one service.

The more data stored, the more carbon removed from (and kept out of!) the atmosphere.
